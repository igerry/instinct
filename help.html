Help and Tips

"Instinct" makes it much easier to resolve puzzles of getting 24. Do not think too much. Trusting your subconsciousness is the key to achieving high scores.

How To Play

Tap the numbers in a puzzle bubble in order. If any four arithmetic operations reacting with numbers in this order can get a result of 24, you win.

The Numbers

You can choose the smaller numbers or the larger numbers. Smaller ones are easier to resolve, while larger numbers generate higher scores. The number will increase gradually according to your performance.

Who Is the Genius

The game provides 4 world ranking results, which are calculated with scores submitted by players, including highest score, largest number of bubbles resolved, and weekly results for these 2 statistics.

How To Improve

The most important tip is to trust your subconsciousness. Do not get stuck in calculating. Just try it without hesitation.

Bubbles And Scores

Number of bubbles resolved shows your speed. Score involves difficulty and represents your overall capability.

The Mascots

Each mascot has specific function, which provides special help in games. While your instinct always matters more than additional help.

In App Purchase

The game is not dependent on these items, but they may bring more choices and fun.

  1. Bonbon: Show more bubbles on the screen. There are 5 bubbles by default, and the bonbon will bring 2 additional bubbles. So that you can get better prepared before start, and have more choices during the game.
  2. Candy: Choose bubble with higher score for you. And thus you are more likely to win higher score.
  3. Chocolate: Provide a hint. It shows the first correct number.
  4. Donut: Automatically calculate the possible combination for you. Clicking 2 numbers resolves 1 puzzle in this case, and you'll get faster and faster.
  5. Max Number: Open all levels for you to choose.

The Icon

Imagine what the cute icon is. It is said that the designer takes dumplings as prototype.

More Tips

Tap other bubble to skip the current one if you get stuck. It is recommended not to stop because combo will generate extra bonus.
